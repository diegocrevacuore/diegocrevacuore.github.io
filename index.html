Diego Crevacuore
Biography

Hi, I'm Diego Crevacuore.
A singer-songwriter, actor and traveler.
A free soul with peace as a compass and
the will to leave the world better than I found it.
I had the opportunity to experience other artistic disciplines such as
painting, photography and acting (on TV, cinema, theatre, and musical plays).
I practised many sports and techniques aimed at physical and spiritual well-being.
I embarked on the adventure of travelling throughout all America: from Ushuaia to Alaska.
I love nature, I defend animals and I collaborate actively with homeless people.
I attended the Buenos Aires University, up to a bachelor’s and master’s degree.
I continue my education and professional training on
piano, guitar, singing and musical production.

My project: keep travelling the world sharing my songs.


"I see life as a gift from God and today my wish is to integrate all my tools and experiences in music: my greatest passion."
